# Safe Response Actions

When an email looks suspicious, follow these steps:

1. Do not click any links or download attachments in the message.
2. Do not reply with any personal, financial, or login information.
3. Hover over links to inspect the true destination before trusting them.
4. Verify the sender through an official, independently obtained channel
   (type the company's website into your browser rather than using the link).
5. If the message impersonates your bank or employer, contact them directly
   using a phone number from their official website or the back of your card.
6. Report the message to your email provider and your organization's security
   team, then delete it.
7. If you already clicked a link or entered credentials, change your password
   immediately and enable multi-factor authentication.

For high-risk messages, isolate the account and notify IT security before
taking any further action.
